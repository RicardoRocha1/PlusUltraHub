Our Purpose

Plus Ultra Hub is meant to be for open science what Github is for open source code. We intend on creating a well-rounded platform with everything a researcher might need, including networking and finding members for projects, a huge array of data and datasets that have been used in research, Github integrated repositories for open source code that might make research work quicker and research publicized by others for reference.

Connecting scientists

A centralized location for researchers to share their expertise and resources, exchange ideas, and work together on projects. This can lead to more efficient and effective research, as well as significant advancements in various fields of study. Additionally, a collaborative platform can help researchers connect with peers who have similar interests and backgrounds, leading to the formation of new partnerships and research groups. It can also facilitate communication between researchers and the wider scientific community, enabling them to share their findings and insights with a larger audience.

Overall, a platform that facilitates scientific collaboration amongst researchers can be a valuable tool that promotes innovation and progress in various fields of study.
